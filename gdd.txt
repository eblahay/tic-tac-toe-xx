Proj_name: Tic-Tac-Toe++
Author: Megatato

summary:
   A text-based console game of tic-tac-toe in which the user inputs
   coordinates and their mark appears on the board in the corresponding
   location. There will also be a singleplayer mode in which a human player
   will face off with a cpu opponent.
